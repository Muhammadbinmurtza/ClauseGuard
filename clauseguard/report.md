## Clause Analysis

### IP_ASSIGNMENT — 🔴 CRITICAL
**Original:** Recipient hereby irrevocably assigns and agrees to assign to Company all right, title, and interest in and to any and all inventions, discoveries, improvements, works of authorship, trade secrets, and other intellectual property conceived, created, or reduced to practice by Recipient, whether alone or with others, during the term of this Agreement and for a period of one (1) year after its termination, regardless of whether such intellectual property was created on Recipient's own time or using Recipient's own equipment.
**Plain English:** You give the company ownership of everything you create, even on your own time with your own equipment, for the entire agreement and one year after it ends.
**Risk:** This clause assigns all IP created during the agreement and for one year after termination, including work done on personal time and with personal equipment, with no carve-out for unrelated personal projects.
**Action:** Negotiate to exclude personal projects created on your own time and with your own equipment, and remove the post-termination assignment period.

### ARBITRATION — 🔴 CRITICAL
**Original:** Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively through binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association in accordance with its Commercial Arbitration Rules. The parties expressly waive any right to a trial by jury and waive the right to participate in any class action or representative proceeding.
**Plain English:** You give up your right to sue in court or join a class action, and any dispute will be decided by a private arbitrator instead.
**Risk:** The clause mandates binding arbitration and explicitly waives the right to a jury trial and the right to participate in any class action or representative proceeding. The combination of mandatory arbitration and a class action waiver is a critical risk.
**Action:** Negotiate to remove the class action waiver or make arbitration optional, allowing you to choose between arbitration and court.

### NON_COMPETE — 🟠 HIGH
**Original:** For a period of eighteen (18) months following the termination of this Agreement, Recipient shall not, directly or indirectly, engage in, own, manage, operate, control, be employed by, consult for, or otherwise provide services to any business that is competitive with the business of Company, anywhere in the world.
**Plain English:** You cannot work for any competing company anywhere in the world for 18 months after this agreement ends.
**Risk:** The non-compete lasts 18 months, which exceeds the standard 1-year period, and applies 'anywhere in the world' with no geographic limitation tied to the Company's actual business footprint.
**Action:** Negotiate to reduce the duration to 12 months and limit the geographic scope to regions where the Company actually operates.

### AUTO_RENEWAL — 🟡 MEDIUM
**Original:** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial term of one (1) year. Thereafter, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.
**Plain English:** This agreement lasts one year and renews automatically unless you give 90 days' notice to stop it.
**Risk:** The agreement auto-renews for successive one-year terms. While the 90-day notice period provides a reasonable window to opt out, the auto-renewal mechanism itself presents a risk of unintentional extension.
